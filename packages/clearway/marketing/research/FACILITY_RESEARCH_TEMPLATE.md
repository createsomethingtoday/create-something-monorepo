## Qualification Criteria

### High Priority (Hot Lead)

Score **3+ Yes** answers:

- [ ] Currently using CourtReserve or similar redirect-based system
- [ ] Website is modern and active
- [ ] Owner/GM active on LinkedIn or social media
- [ ] 4-16 courts (sweet spot for CLEARWAY)
- [ ] Located in DFW metro area
- [ ] Evidence of tech-forward approach (online booking, social media, etc.)

### Medium Priority (Warm Lead)

Score **2 Yes** answers:

- [ ] Has a website (even if basic)
- [ ] Currently using manual booking (phone/email)
- [ ] 2-8 courts
- [ ] Within 50 miles of DFW

### Low Priority (Cold Lead)

Score **0-1 Yes** answers:

- [ ] No website or outdated website
- [ ] No online presence
- [ ] Very high volume (>100 bookings/month) - flat fee might be better
- [ ] Complex membership tiers (CLEARWAY Phase 1 doesn't support yet)

## Contact Discovery Methods

### Method 1: Website Contact Page

1. Visit facility website
2. Look for "Contact Us" page
3. Find email address (often info@, reservations@, or owner@domain.com)
4. Note: Generic emails are okay for cold outreach

### Method 2: LinkedIn Search

1. Search: `"[Facility Name]" owner OR manager`
2. Filter by location (DFW metro)
3. Look for profiles with facility in job title
4. Connect with personalized message OR find email via LinkedIn profile

### Method 3: Google Business Profile

1. Search: `[Facility Name] + location`
2. Check Google Business listing
3. Phone number usually listed
4. Sometimes email in "About" section

### Method 4: Domain WHOIS Lookup

1. Use whois.com or similar
2. Enter facility website domain
3. Check registrant email (often owner/manager)
4. Note: May be privacy-protected

### Method 5: Social Media

1. Check Facebook, Instagram for facility
2. Look for "About" section with contact info
3. Check recent posts for staff mentions
4. DM facility page if no email found

### Method 6: Local Business Directories

1. Yelp, Yellow Pages, local chamber of commerce
2. Search facility name
3. Check business listing for contact info

## How to Use This Template

### Step 1: Build Target List
1. Google "pickleball facilities DFW"
2. Check Yelp, Google Maps, local directories
3. Add facilities to table above

### Step 2: Research Each Facility
1. Copy "Research Notes Template" section
2. Fill in details for each facility
3. Assign priority (High/Medium/Low)

### Step 3: Find Decision Maker
1. Use Contact Discovery Methods
2. Prioritize LinkedIn (more personal) or email (scalable)
3. Verify email using Hunter.io or similar

### Step 4: Personalize Outreach
1. Use findings to customize email template
2. Mention specific detail (e.g., "I saw you have 8 courts")
3. Reference current system if known (e.g., "I noticed you're using CourtReserve")

### Step 5: Track Progress
1. Log outreach in tracking spreadsheet (see OUTREACH_TRACKING.md)
2. Update status as conversations progress
3. Note any insights for future outreach

## Red Flags (Disqualify)

Skip facilities with these signals:

- [ ] No website and no online presence
- [ ] Facility closed or out of business
- [ ] Private club (not open to public bookings)
- [ ] Explicitly states "No online booking" in marketing
- [ ] Located outside DFW metro area (for Phase 1)
